Our Services

Women in Tech® operates on four pillars: education, business, digital inclusion, and advocacy. These pillars support the organization’s comprehensive approach to equipping women with the skills necessary for STEAM careers, supporting women entrepreneurs and leaders, ensuring digital access for all, and driving systemic change to challenge gender biases in the tech industry.
